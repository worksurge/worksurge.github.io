Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 2-month free trial work?

After signing up, your company has free reign to play with all of our Worksurge features for two months free. After your free trial is up, we'll automatically bill your credit card on file each month.

Are there any contracts or long-term agreements?

Nope! With Worksurge, canceling your account is as simple as clicking a button.

Do I need to download or install anything on my computers?

Nope! No installation or coding is necessary. Worksurge is completely plug and play on any modern web browser. Smartphone and tablet apps are coming soon.

What happens at the end of my trial?

At the end of your trial, you will be billed automatically each month to the credit card on file for the number of active users on your account.

Do I, the owner count as an employee?

Yes, all active employees in Worksurge count toward your plan. Inactive and terminated employees do not.

How does consolidated billing work for all of my SaaS applications?

We have partnered with some of the top SaaS applications ranging from CRM to collaboration tools, analytics software to human resource applications. All current billing is transferred to your Worksurge account. Any new apps added will also be billed in your monthly Worksurge invoice. Receipts with a detailed breakdown can always be found in the Billing tab of the admin settings.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We currently accept Mastercard, Visa, American Express, and Discover.

How secure is my data?

Your data and files are stored under SSL encryption both to and from your browser. They're also stored fully encrypted in our database and on our servers.
